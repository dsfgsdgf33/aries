## What are you working on?
I'm building Aries — a fully self-hosted AI platform with zero npm dependencies, an autonomous coding agent, and a P2P swarm compute network. It's open source (MIT) and gives anyone complete ownership of their AI tools without depending on corporate subscriptions or cloud services.

## What is unique or novel about your project?
Three things that nobody else has done together:

1. **Zero dependencies**: Aries is built entirely on Node.js built-ins — no npm packages whatsoever. This eliminates supply chain attacks, reduces bloat, and proves you can build complex software without the dependency treadmill. No other AI platform has attempted this.

2. **P2P swarm compute**: Instead of centralized GPU farms, Aries distributes AI inference across community machines. Machines that can't run inference fall back to crypto mining. Everyone participates in the compute economy.

3. **Autonomous coding agent**: Aries Code doesn't just autocomplete — it autonomously plans, builds, tests, and debugs complete applications.

## How will this improve the world?
AI is becoming the most important technology of our lifetime, but it's controlled by a handful of corporations. Users pay subscriptions, surrender their data, and depend on infrastructure they don't own. Aries democratizes AI by making it self-hosted, community-powered, and free. The P2P swarm means even people with modest hardware can participate in and benefit from the AI economy.
