# Aries — Master Pitch Document

## One-Liner
Aries is a self-hosted, zero-dependency AI platform with autonomous coding, P2P swarm compute, and crypto mining — putting AI ownership back in users' hands.

## The Problem
AI is locked behind corporate gatekeepers. Users pay monthly subscriptions, surrender their data, and depend on centralized infrastructure that can be censored, throttled, or shut down. There is no privacy, no ownership, no sovereignty. Meanwhile, billions of dollars of consumer compute sits idle.

## The Solution: Aries
Aries is a fully self-hosted AI platform built entirely on Node.js built-ins — **zero npm dependencies** (genuinely unique in the Node.js ecosystem). It includes:

- **Aries Code** — An autonomous coding agent that plans, builds, tests, and fixes entire applications end-to-end
- **P2P Swarm Compute Network** — Distributed AI inference and training across community machines
- **Crypto Mining Fallback** — Low-end machines that can't run inference contribute via mining, ensuring everyone participates in the compute economy
- **Cyberpunk Web Dashboard** — Beautiful UI with 42+ integrated tools, multi-provider AI support (OpenAI, Anthropic, local models, etc.)

## Why It's Different
1. **Zero Dependencies**: No npm packages. Built entirely on Node.js built-ins. This means no supply chain attacks, no dependency hell, no bloat. No one else has done this at this scale.
2. **True Self-Hosting**: Everything runs on your hardware. Your data never leaves your machine.
3. **Community Compute Economy**: The P2P swarm lets anyone contribute compute and earn, democratizing AI infrastructure.
4. **Autonomous Coding**: Aries Code doesn't just assist — it autonomously plans, implements, tests, and debugs complete applications.
